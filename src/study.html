Online Class

An online class is a virtual learning environment where you can gain new skills or knowledge from anywhere in the world. It typically includes video lectures, assignments, and quizzes, allowing you to learn at your own pace. Perfect for those looking to upskill or explore new topics.

MBTI

The MBTI (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator) test is a personality assessment that helps you understand your preferences in how you think, interact with others, and make decisions. It categorizes people into 16 personality types, offering insights into personal strengths, career paths, and relationships.
